About The Campus
Ambassador Program

The ‘Campus Ambassador Program’ by Entrepreneurship-cell (E-Cell), is designed to engage and empower students of different colleges in various impactful initiatives. This program aims to foster a mutually beneficial relationship between our organisation and talented individuals who demonstrate leadership potential and a passion for promoting our mission.

Vision

Creating a powerful alliance between our organization and the student community.

Mission

To foster a positive environment of collaboration, innovation, and social responsibility.
